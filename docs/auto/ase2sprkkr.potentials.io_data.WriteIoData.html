WriteIoData¶

Full name: ase2sprkkr.potentials.io_data.WriteIoData

class ase2sprkkr.potentials.io_data.WriteIoData(atoms)[source]¶

Bases: ase2sprkkr.potentials.io_data.BaseIoData

During writing of potential file, some lists are needed in more sections, typically one section contains the list itself, while another(s) reference(s) the items of the list. So the lists are created on demand and cached for its further use.
